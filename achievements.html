Golden Octave Award 2024

Golden Octave is a prestigious music competition where music group all over Singapore goes to compete and stand a chance to present their piece after clearing a difficult entrance to the shortlisted groups.

We managed to work hard and make it to the shortlisted groups to compete for the Golden Octave Award.

After months of effort practising to get the Golden Octave Award, we gave it our all during the competition and we were happy that we have achieved merit for the competition.

Symphonic Sounds Award 2026

Symphonic Sounds is a music orchestra competition with highlighting the orchestra culture from all over singapore.

With the guidance of our alumni, we prepared our piece for the competition and spent alot of time practising for it.

Despite the fierce competition, we managed to score 2nd place with the cooperation of everyone.

Magic Melody Award 2023

Magic Melody is a large scale music competition where groups of musician come together and perform a piece dedicated to a certain theme.

The theme we got was harmonic friendship and we came up with a piece with the teamwork of the alumni and the club members.

We performed our piece and managed to score first place!
