What are the CCA dates?

CCA is on different days, depending on your schedule. On Mondays, every member is required to attend. However, you also have to attend sectionals, which is on another day of the week depending on what your CCA is.

How do I apply for this CCA?

You can click here to get redirected to the sign-up form.

If I played an instrument prior to joining this CCA, am I able to continue playing it?

Yes, you are able to do so. Simply contact one of the teachers-in-charge within one month of joining this CCA.

If I am unable to attend one day of CCA due to a commitment to another CCA, what do I do?

You are required to inform the teachers-in-charge as soon as possible in order to make the necessary arrangements.

Am I required to pick my instrument, or will it be determined by a trial?

If you know how to play an instrument prior to joining NYP's Symphony Orchestra, you may opt to play said instrument. Otherwise, you will be required to attend a trial. You will be informed of the trial date via email.
