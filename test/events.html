Performance at Victoria Concert Hall

Date: 20 August 2025
Time: 3pm - 5pm
Location: Victoria Concert Hall

Come one, come all to this magnificent event hosted once every two years! Our orchestra will be performing some timeless classical pieces, such as Beethoven's Moonlight Sonata and Bach's Toccata and Fugue in D Minor. This is a rare opportunity to both showcase our orchestra's capability and potential, as well as for you to support your fellow schoolmates! Mark your calendars, because we will ensure that this is a performance you will never forget!

CCA Open House

Date: 2 September 2025
Time: 10am - 3pm
Location: E-222 and E-223

Are YOU interested in joining the NYP Symphony Orchestra's family? Do you want ot learn how to play an instrument, even without any prior musical training! Well', you're in luck! This CCA Open House will give you all the experience you need to get started with us- and maybe become a great musician someday! This Open House allows you to get a glimpse into how NYP's Symphony Orchestra goes about its sessions, and it gives you some hands-on experience too! After all this, you can feel free to apply to join our family and learn an instrument you will undoubtedly excel in!

Collaboration with XYZ School Orchestra

Date: 30 September 2025
Time: 2pm - 4pm
Location: Marina Bay Sands

NYP's Symphony Orchestra is collaborating with XYZ's Orchestra in the form of a memorable performance! Located in Marina Bay Sands' Event Plaza, this event will take place sometime in the afternoon. Bringing to you unforgettable classics such as Mozart's Requiem, this collaboration aims to broaden our students' scope of the world and open their eyes to the lives of others they may not be familiar with, all while showcasing how, when words fail, music always prevails. This will certainly be a good use of your afternoon!
